Selected Projects

Countdown Logistics Optimisation
A cost-efficient trucking logistics plan was required for transporting specific palletes of goods from distribution centres to Countdown stores across NZ. Using demand data, travel data and trucking constraints, stochastic modelling and linear programs were designed to meet the objective. A final solution costing approximately $136730 per week is proposed.

Airfoil Performance Modelling
A small commercial aircraft similar to a Cessna 172 was analysed and optimised for flight reliability using Ansys. Wing spar dimensions and airfoil configurations were iteratively modified and evaluated for structural, aerodynamic, and thermal performance. The optimal design features a 120 mm × 140 mm spar, 4 mm skin thickness, five ribs, and a 5° angle of attack. However, the proposed anti-icing strategy was unsuccessful and requires further investigation.
Computational Modelling Ansys Software Parameter Optimisation
Geothermal Reservoir Optimisation
This project requires recommending a safe injection rate for a local goethermal reservoir that balances economic benefits with environmental, cultural, and stakeholder concerns while keeping seismic activity below regulatory limits. This involved developing a mathematical ODE model of the relationship between geothermal water injection rates, reservoir pressure, and induced seismicity using time-series data of the pressure and injection rate as well as academic research. The result is a recommended 175.5 tonne/hour injection rate.
Python Mathematical Modelling
Simplex Algorithm from scratch
The Revised Simplex Method (RSM) is built from scratch able to efficiently solve Linear Programming problems. The RSM solver was constructed using MATLAB R2024b, successfully maximising or minimising a linear objective functions subject to linear equality and inequality constraints. This can be applied to resource allocation, production planning, transportation and logisitcs, network flows and financial and economic optimisation.
